Seminars

The research unit regularly organizes research seminars and workshops on topics related to our research. The presentations are usually attended by 30 to 40 researchers and include brainstorming sessions. Seminars are usually held on Friday from 11:00 to 12:00 in room R7, Montefiore Institute (B28).

The details of the upcomming presentations can be accessed below. Please note that some links might require you to login (available only to the research unit members).
